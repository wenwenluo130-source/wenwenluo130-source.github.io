Ethylene Tar Market Report

Turning Industry Data into Strategic Content

In 2025, I led the research and writing of a 12,900-word annual market report on ethylene tar, designed for industry practitioners who rely on data to make purchasing and investment decisions. The biggest challenge was data reliability. Production capacity data from suppliers is often incomplete or confidential. To solve this, I built a dual-source validation system—conducting phone interviews with more than 20 producers and 50 downstream companies to cross-check real market circulation. Another challenge was content clarity. Industry reports can easily become dense and difficult to read. So I translated raw data into 15+ structured charts, and integrated client concerns—such as policy changes and price forecasts—into the analysis. The final result was a 25-page commercial report, covering the entire industry chain and delivering a 2026 price forecast. This project reflects how I approach content strategy: turning complex data into clear, decision-ready insights.

Galand Film Week

Solving User Demand Through Content Design

1,698 followers, yet a 26% engagement rate—260 likes, 101 saves, and 80 comments. The challenge was clear: With a low-weight account, zero budget, and the National Day holiday approaching, how do we make target users actively search, save, and join? First, user segmentation： We categorized users by emotional motivation—such as thinkers seeking depth and fun-seekers looking for relaxation—and matched each group with tailored content. Second, shareability design： We created a social-friendly slogan and a strong visual hook, so users weren't just saving information—they were saving something worth sharing. Third, lightweight UGC fission： Through a "Film Talker" mechanism, users became co-creators, enabling organic reach with zero cost. This framework works well for holiday marketing, local lifestyle campaigns, community cold starts, and zero-budget growth.

Cover Letter AI Tool

A Builder Story About Content Workflow

While exploring freelance platforms like Upwork, I noticed a common problem： Freelancers often have less than 30 minutes to apply before a job post becomes crowded. Yet writing a strong cover letter still requires understanding the job description, structuring the message, and highlighting personal advantages. Many people try AI tools—but they end up spending even more time writing prompts and revising drafts. So I decided to build a simple solution. Instead of focusing on AI writing alone, I focused on content workflow design. I mapped the user journey—from job post to proposal—and turned it into a five-step workflow: capturing context, adjusting tone, generating drafts, reviewing with AI, and exporting the final message. During development, I also built a simple debugging workflow using GitHub and Vercel to test, deploy, and fix issues quickly. What started as a small tool became a lesson in turning user pain points into structured content systems—a mindset I apply to content strategy, product storytelling, and AI-driven content experiences.

乙烯焦油市场报告

将行业数据转化为战略内容

2025 年，我主导完成了一份12900 字乙烯焦油年度市场报告的全流程调研与撰写，报告面向依托数据进行采购与投资决策的行业专业人士。 项目核心难点在于数据可靠性：企业产能信息普遍不完整，且核心数据多属商业机密。为此，我建立双源交叉验证体系，通过电话访谈20 余家生产企业与50 余家下游企业，对市场真实供需与流通情况进行交叉核验。 另一挑战是提升报告可读性：行业报告常因内容庞杂而晦涩难懂。我将原始数据转化为15 + 份结构化图表，并将政策变化，价格预测等客户核心关切点融入分析框架。 最终形成25 页商用正式报告，完整覆盖全产业链分析，并提出2026 年价格预测。 该项目体现了我的内容策略能力，能够将复杂数据转化为清晰、可直接支撑决策的专业洞察。

求职信 AI 工具

一位内容工作流程创作者的故事

在浏览 Upwork 等自由职业平台时，我发现了一个普遍痛点： 用户申请岗位需要发送求职信，但投递黄金窗口期往往不足 30 分钟，一旦错过，申请的成功率会迅速降低。 可一封有说服力的求职信，既要精准解读岗位需求、梳理信息结构，又要突出个人核心优势，本就耗时耗力。 不少人尝试借助 AI 工具提速，却反而陷入反复编写提示词、修改初稿的内耗中，效率不升反降。 于是我决定打造一款轻量化解决方案： 我没有局限于 AI 写作本身，而是将重心放在内容工作流程的设计上。 我完整梳理了用户从看到招聘信息到提交申请的全流程，将其提炼为一套五步标准化流程：捕捉背景信息、调整表达语气、生成初稿、AI 辅助审核、导出最终文案。 开发过程中，我还借助 GitHub 与 Vercel 搭建了简易调试流程，实现快速测试、部署与问题修复，保障工具高效迭代。 这款网站的制作经历，让我真正掌握了将用户痛点转化为结构化内容系统的核心能力。此外，我也能够将这套思维灵活运用到内容策略、产品叙事，以及 AI 驱动的内容体验设计等工作中。
